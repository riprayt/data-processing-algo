Explanation

Price is fixed at $5. The greedy strategy prioritizes larger bills for change (50 → 20 → 10 → 5), which ensures 10+5 for $15 and favors $20s for $45 while using only one $5 when possible. Preserving $5 bills is critical: without $5s, future change becomes impossible. You can now seed the register using the bill:count field; if left blank it starts empty. Customers are processed in order.
